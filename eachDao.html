What is Oasis.app?

Oasis.app is the frontend to access Maker Protocol and create Dai. Oasis.app aims to be the most trusted entry point to the Maker Protocol. The long term mission is to allow users to simply and easily deploy their capital into DeFi and manage it in one trusted place.

Maker Protocol is comprised of a decentralized stablecoin, collateral loans, and community governance.
